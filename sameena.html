Ms. Sameena P

Remedial Teacher | De Beacon

Ms. Sameena P is a dedicated Remedial Teacher with a strong foundation in addressing learning difficulties among children. Having completed the Certified Academic Backwardness Trainers Training (ABTT) program under the University of Calicut, she brings both theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience to her role at De Beacon.

Position: Remedial Teacher
Institution: De Beacon
Certification: ABTT – University of Calicut
Experience: Specialized in individualized remedial training

Career Milestones

  • Certified Academic Backwardness Trainer (ABTT) from the University of Calicut.
  • Experienced in individualized remedial training and academic support.
  • Passionate about empowering children with learning challenges through patience and personalized attention.
  • Skilled in classroom engagement, adaptability, and innovative teaching techniques.

Educational Qualification

  • ABTT – Certified Academic Backwardness Trainers Training, University of Calicut
  • DMLT – Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology

Professional Profile

Sameena P has successfully supported children facing academic and concentration-related challenges, helping them regain confidence and measurable progress in their studies. Her compassionate and patient approach enables her to tailor instruction to each learner’s unique needs.

During her internship at Markaz IHRAM, she gained valuable experience in individualized remedial intervention, focusing on empowering children through structured and empathetic educational methods.

Certifications & Training

  • Trainee – Program on Remedial Teaching, CARE IEP Centre (August 2025)

Vision

“To help every child overcome their learning barriers through consistent guidance, empathy, and innovative remedial strategies — empowering them to believe in their own abilities and achieve lasting progress.”
