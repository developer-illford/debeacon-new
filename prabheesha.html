Ms. Prabheesha V.E

Family & Adult Psychologist | Psychiatric Social Worker – De Beacon

Ms. Prabheesha V. E is a compassionate Family and Adult Psychologist with a strong background in clinical and psychiatric social work. She focuses on helping clients overcome stress, anxiety, and family conflicts through evidence-based therapeutic methods.

Specialization: Family & Couple Therapy, Psychotherapy
Experience: Clinical & Hospital Settings
Position: Psychologist & Social Worker

Career Milestones

  • Experienced Mental Health Professional with a strong clinical and psychiatric background.
  • Former Counsellor at Medical College & Beach Hospital, Kozhikode.
  • Specialized in Family & Couple Therapy, Psychotherapy, and Rehabilitation.
  • Expertise in managing Addiction, Stress, Anger, and Emotional Regulation.
  • Dedicated to helping individuals and families build resilience and well-being.

Educational Qualification

  • M.Phil. in Psychiatric Social Work – Central Institute of Psychiatry
  • Master of Social Work (MSW) – Sree Sankaracharya University of Sanskrit, Kalady

Professional Profile

Ms. Prabheesha has worked in hospital and community settings, providing support for individuals facing psychological, behavioral, and emotional challenges. At De Beacon, she empowers clients through psychotherapy, family systems therapy, and motivational techniques, helping them rebuild confidence and emotional balance.

Areas of Specialization

  • Psychosocial Management of Clients
  • Addiction Recovery & Rehabilitation
  • Family & Couple Therapy
  • Supportive Psychotherapy
  • Anger & Stress Management
  • Motivation Enhancement & Relapse Prevention

Vision

“Healing begins when understanding meets empathy. My goal is to guide individuals toward clarity, strength, and lasting emotional balance.”

At De Beacon, we provide compassionate care and structured guidance to help individuals and families achieve emotional well-being and resilience.
