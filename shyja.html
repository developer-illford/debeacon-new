Ms. Shyja N.K

Remedial Expert | Early Childhood & Learning Development Specialist – De Beacon

With over 20 years of experience in early childhood education and remedial training, Ms. Shyja is a dedicated professional in child development, focusing on individualized teaching strategies to help children overcome learning difficulties.

Experience: 20+ Years
Position: Remedial Expert
Specialization: Montessori & ALDI Curriculum

Career Milestones

  • Over 20 years of experience in early childhood education and remedial teaching.
  • Expert in identifying and addressing learning difficulties among children.
  • Skilled in Montessori and ALDI curriculum-based teaching methods.
  • Passionate about guiding children toward academic confidence and emotional growth.
  • Known for her child-centered, empathetic, and structured teaching approach.

Educational Qualification

  • DECE – Diploma in Early Childhood Care and Education
  • DMTT – Diploma in Montessori Teacher Training
  • NTTC – Nursery Teacher Training Course
  • CTTP – Computer Teacher Training Programme
  • B.A. Economics

Professional Experience

Ms. Shyja has served at Stella Maris Boarding School, Healing Light International Training Centre, and Face International School. At De Beacon, she leads the Remedial Coaching & Mentoring Division, focusing on literacy, numeracy, and learning enhancement programs tailored for each child.

  • Remedial Expert, De Beacon – 2024–Present
  • K.G Teacher, Stella Maris Boarding School – June 2024–Present
  • Montessori Trainer & Remedial Teacher, Healing Light International Training Centre – Mar 2021–Mar 2024
  • K.G Teacher, Face International School – Jun 2006–Mar 2020

Additional Training & Certifications

  • Certified in ALDI Curriculum for preschool teaching
  • Life Enrichment Training (LET) and Transactional Analysis (TA) at Markaz IHRAM Karanthu
  • Participated in District-level Capacity Building Workshop by Bharath Sevak Samaj

Vision
