Basic Remedial Coaching (BRC) - 15 Days

Our Basic Remedial Coaching program is designed for students who need short-term, focused support to address their learning difficulties. The journey begins with a comprehensive assessment and counseling session, where our experts identify specific academic challenges using structured tools and provide personalized feedback to both parents and students. Based on this evaluation, we create a targeted improvement plan. Students then receive 15 one-to-one remedial sessions with a remedial expert (45 minutes each), where individualized strategies are applied to strengthen foundational skills. This short-term yet impactful program helps students gain clarity, confidence, and improved learning habits.

Advanced Post-Remedial Coaching (APRC) – 1 Year

The Advanced Post-Remedial Coaching program is specially designed for students who have already completed basic remedial training (minimum 15 days) and are now ready to move to the next level of academic mastery. This year-long program provides structured, individualized coaching that focuses on consolidating foundational learning while introducing advanced academic strategies.

  • Online Students: One-to-one sessions, 1 hour per week (approximately 48–50 sessions annually).
  • Offline Students: Bi-weekly one-to-one sessions (1 hour each) at our De Beacon Koduvally office or online, plus a monthly practical-oriented group training day led by specialized trainers.

The program enhances comprehension, problem-solving, and critical thinking while improving self-regulation, time management, and independent learning. Regular assessments and ongoing feedback ensure continuous progress tracking.
